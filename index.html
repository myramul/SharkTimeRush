Help

Game Objective:

Your goal in Shark Time Rush is to control the shark and avoid obstacles for as long as possible. The longer you survive without hitting an obstacle, the higher your score will be. The game has themed levels with increasing difficulty that the player comes across as their score climbs.

Game Controls:

  • [SPACE or W]: Jump
  • [DOWN or S]: Duck
  • [X]: Pause(Menu) and Resume
